    TERMS & CONDITIONS

    • To participate in Pre-ICO you will need an Ethereum wallet client in your browser e.g., Metamask.
    • All purchasers will be accepted in ETH only.
    • If you have an alternative cryptocurrency e.g., BTC or fiat currency e.g., GBP, you will need to convert these to ETH using a reputable exchange e.g., coinbase and transfer the ETH to your Ethereum wallet.
    • Prices for ETH will be updated on the Pre-ICO webpage using a live feed from www.cryptocompare.com.
    • All deposits received in ETH will be held in a corporate grade BitGo wallet with multi-signature authentication.
    • To take part in the Pre-ICO you must send an amount of ETH to the Crowdsale Address on this webpage.
    • After successfully sending ETH, you will be issued with a confirmation receipt by email notifying you of the amount of Medici Tokens you have purchased.
    • All Medici Tokens purchased during the Pre-ICO will be transferred to your Ethereum Wallet during the Token Generation Event (TGE) later this year.
